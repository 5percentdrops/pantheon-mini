# Hermes Learning Policy

Hermes starts from seed skills, then evolves.

Candidate skill:
- one strong success,
- one clear post-mortem lesson,
- or one repeated failure pattern.

Promoted skill:
- 3 to 5 repeated wins,
- statistically meaningful improvement over baseline,
- or explicit approval by the Project Manager / relevant Senior Advisor.

Archived skill:
- underperforms baseline for 3 consecutive uses,
- causes compliance/security/quality problems,
- or no longer matches the house operating model.
