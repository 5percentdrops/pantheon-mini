# NotebookLM / Custom Wiki Research Workflow

## Owner
Owen — Research Pack Agent

## Purpose
Use NotebookLM and the user's custom wiki to create a research-backed first PRD.

## Inputs
- raw idea
- NotebookLM workspace/export
- custom wiki
- uploaded documents
- source lists
- public resources

## Outputs
- 50-resource research pack
- source summaries
- what/how summary
- tools/APIs/libraries discovered
- competitors/examples
- first PRD draft
- open questions

## Rule
Owen builds the evidence base. Owen does not decide feasibility.
