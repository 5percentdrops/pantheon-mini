# Paperclip Control Plane Policy

Paperclip is the company/control plane for the Pantheon Mini.

Paperclip owns:

- company/org structure
- goals
- issues
- work assignment
- heartbeats
- budgets
- audit trail
- approvals
- pause/terminate controls
- merge/deploy gates

Arthur is not Paperclip itself. Arthur is the Project Manager / Head employee inside the Paperclip company.

Hermes is not Paperclip. Hermes is the harness/runtime that runs an underlying LLM model with tools, memory, skills and sessions.

## Hard rules

- Paperclip may assign work.
- Paperclip may enforce budgets and gates.
- Paperclip may stage import payloads.
- Paperclip must not bypass human approval for governance, merge, deployment, production trading rules, or API-key permissions.
