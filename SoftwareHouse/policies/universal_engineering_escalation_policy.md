# Universal Engineering Escalation Policy

## Scope
This policy applies to all Software House engineering work.

## Standard developer attempt rule
Every standard developer has 7-10 self-fix attempts.

If still blocked, they send a blocker packet to their senior owner.

## Senior developer attempt rule
Every senior developer has 3 diagnosis/fix cycles.

If unresolved, they escalate to Arthur.

## Principal / code escalation rule
Arthur decides escalation dispatch:
- Cody receives the code/debug/security packet.
- Magnus receives the approach/API/library/scalability/security/reliability packet.

## Routing rule
Magnus reports to Arthur.
Cody reports to Arthur.
Arthur selects the route.
The relevant senior developer rewrites the plan/checklist.
The standard developer executes.

## Hard rule
No standard developer may bypass their senior owner and go directly to Magnus or Cody.
